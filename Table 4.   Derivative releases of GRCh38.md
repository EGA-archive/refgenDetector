GRCh38\_no\_alt\_plus-hs38d1 is differentiated by 2313 decoy contings not present in the original release of GRCh38. They contain unlocalized and unplaced scaffolds  from the GRCh38 Primary Assembly unit (NCBI 2022v2). hs38dh\_extra, in addition to decoy, has multiple alleles of HLA genes as ALT contigs. These genomic sequences are key to represent the high sequence divergence between the HLA complex and the reference genome in these regions (Li 2013). The length of all the HLA contigs is similar, as they represent the same localization. Therefore, differentiation by length is not possible for this particular flavor. It is noteworthy that hs38dh\_extra identification is the sole flavor that depends on the nomenclature of the contigs.
